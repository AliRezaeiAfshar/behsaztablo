Our Journey of Innovation

From a small electrical engineering firm to a leading manufacturer of critical infrastructure solutions.

1999 - Company Founded

Electrical engineer Robert Chen establishes ElectroPanel Pro with a vision to revolutionize industrial electrical distribution.

2003 - Healthcare Breakthrough

Completed first major hospital emergency power system, establishing our reputation in critical infrastructure.

2008 - Manufacturing Expansion

Opened 50,000 sq ft manufacturing facility with advanced testing laboratory and quality control systems.

2015 - Industry Recognition

Achieved UL listing, NEMA membership, and ISO 9001:2015 certification, setting new quality standards.

2020 - Digital Innovation

Launched advanced monitoring systems and IoT-enabled panels for predictive maintenance capabilities.

2025 - Continued Excellence

500+ projects completed with zero safety incidents, serving critical infrastructure nationwide.
